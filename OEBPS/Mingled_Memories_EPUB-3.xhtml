Instructions

The document is published in EPUB3 format and is enriched with animations and files that are linked to the Quick Response codes that appear in the text. The files are stored in the blog edgedpub.com and the code files are available in the reopository github.com/edgedpub/Mingled-Memories.

• To read the text, scroll downwards or sidewards

• To see the illustrations and the QR codes, click on the button

• To view the AR images, scan the QR codes with your smartphone or tablet

• The 3D models that appear on the screen can be moved and pinched in and out with your fingers
