Introduction

The following work is the result of a practice-based research study on the performative aesthetics of enhanced e-books conducted by Daniele Giampà in 2021. The aim of the project is to show and to explain how the aesthetics of the User Interface is generatedby the code and to demonstrate the characteristics and the functionalities of the performative materiality of a digital text.

For this reason the work was created with Open Source technology:

- the code files are taken from Codepen and are written in HTML, CSS and JavaScript;

- the technology used for the Augmented Reality images is Three.js

- the 3D models are retrived from Sketchfab and Turbosquid and edited by Jelena Dukić.
